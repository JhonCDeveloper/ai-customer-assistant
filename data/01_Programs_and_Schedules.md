# Colombia Lingo Academy - Course Catalog and Schedules (2026)

## 1. Available Languages and Levels
We offer courses in English, Spanish for Foreigners, and French. All our programs follow the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR). 
Available levels: A1 (Beginner), A2 (Elementary), B1 (Intermediate), B2 (Upper Intermediate), and C1 (Advanced). We currently do not offer C2 level courses.

## 2. Study Modalities
Students can choose from three different study modalities:
* In-Person: Available at our main campus in Medellin (El Poblado) and our branch in Bogota (Chapinero).
* Online Live: Classes taught in real-time via Zoom with live instructors.
* Online Asynchronous: Self-paced learning through our web platform with pre-recorded videos and weekly tutor check-ins.

## 3. Class Schedules
We offer three schedule formats for In-Person and Online Live modalities:
* Intensive Program: Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM (20 hours per week).
* Regular Program: Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM (6 hours per week).
* Weekend Program: Saturdays only, from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM (5 hours per week).
Asynchronous students have 24/7 access to the platform and can study at their own pace.
