# Colombia Lingo Academy - Pricing and Enrollment Guide

## 1. Enrollment Process
To join Colombia Lingo Academy, prospective students must follow these three steps:
1. Placement Test: Take our free online placement test on our website to determine your current CEFR level. (Skip this step if you are starting from A1).
2. Registration: Fill out the online enrollment form with your personal details and chosen modality/schedule.
3. Payment: Complete the payment for the registration fee and the first month of tuition.

## 2. Pricing Structure (2026)
All prices are listed in US Dollars (USD).
* One-time Registration Fee: $45 USD (Mandatory for all new students, covers digital materials).
* Intensive Program Tuition: $220 USD per month.
* Regular Program Tuition: $140 USD per month.
* Weekend Program Tuition: $120 USD per month.
* Online Asynchronous Tuition: $75 USD per month.

## 3. Payment Methods and Refunds
We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), PayPal, and direct bank transfers via PSE (for Colombian bank accounts). 
Refund Policy: Full refunds are only issued if requested at least 48 hours before the course start date. After the course begins, no refunds are provided, but students can freeze their account for up to 3 months for a $15 USD administrative fee.
