# Colombia Lingo Academy - Evaluation and Certification Policies

## 1. Attendance Requirements
To successfully pass a level and be eligible for certification, students must maintain a minimum attendance rate:
* In-Person and Online Live courses require 80% attendance.
* Students who miss more than 3 consecutive classes without a valid medical excuse will be marked as "Failed due to absence."
* Asynchronous students must complete 100% of the platform modules to pass.

## 2. Evaluation System
Each level lasts exactly 8 weeks. Students are evaluated based on:
* Mid-term Exam (Week 4): Covers grammar and listening (40% of final grade).
* Final Exam (Week 8): Covers speaking, reading, and writing (60% of final grade).
The passing grade is 7.0 out of 10.0. If a student scores between 6.0 and 6.9, they can take a make-up exam for an additional fee of $25 USD.

## 3. Certifications
Upon successful completion of any level, students receive a digital Certificate of Completion at no extra cost, sent to their email within 5 business days.
* Printed Diplomas: Students can request a physical, stamped diploma for a fee of $20 USD (shipping included within Colombia).
* International Exams: While we provide internal certifications, we are also an official preparation center for IELTS and TOEFL. We do not administer these international exams directly, but we offer a 10% discount on preparation courses for active C1 students.
